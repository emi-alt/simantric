Different typs of animals

On Earth, we have got different kinds of animals such as aquatic, terrestrial and aerial animals.

Terrestrial Animals

Terrestrial animals are the animals that live on land. These include animals such as cat, dog, cow, tiger, donkey, etc.
Then these are also divided into sub categories such as wild animals, domestic animals, carnivores, herbivores etc.

We find wild animals that prey on other animals examples of which are tigers, lions, bear, etc. We also find animals that can be kept at home with us to give us company such as cats, dogs, rabbits, parrots, etc. And we also find animals that give us food products and can be used for transport and travels by reering them, their examples are cow, donkey, goat, chicken, etc.

Aquatic Animals

Aquatic animals are the animals that live under water, in water bodies like ocean, lakes, rivers. Examples of these are fishes, octopus, sharks, whales, etc.

Aerial Animals

Aerial animals are the animals that fly in the sky and have nests as home. These animals are birds
